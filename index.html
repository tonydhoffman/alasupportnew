Administrators

An Administrator is a user with additional permissions. Administrators can add, edit, delete users. Unlike users, Administrators have access to the billing information and the ability to setup Image Packages. Administrators may use Alacourt in the same way as an Alacourt User.

Users

Users have access to Alacourt, however, they can not access the Administrator functions. Users cannot add, edit, delete users or change billing information or the ability to setup Image Packages. They can update their information and use Alacourt in the same way as an Alacourt Administrator.
